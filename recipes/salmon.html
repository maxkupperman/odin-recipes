Oven-roasted Salmon

Description

A luxurious weeknight favorite of mine, this dish was the staple meal of my childhood. It's also ridiculously simple. Serve with roasted brussels sprouts and rice for full effect.

Ingredients

  • 1 filet salmon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Line a baking pan with aluminum foil. Add the filet to the center.
  3. Drizzle olive oil onto the filet. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook salmon in oven until internal temperature reaches 60C, or approx. 12-15 minutes. You may need to take longer depending on the size of the filet.
  5. Remove from oven and serve.
